# ConDyn Signal Architecture

## Structural Signal Philosophy

Signals are not raw events.

Signals are higher-order structural detections
emerging from:

    events
    propagation
    topology
    density
    instability
    relation movement

Signals represent:

    structural interpretation layers

Signals describe:

- topology behavior
- propagation behavior
- authority movement
- workflow compression
- instability formation
- escalation density
- bottleneck emergence

Signals are:

    organizational field indicators
