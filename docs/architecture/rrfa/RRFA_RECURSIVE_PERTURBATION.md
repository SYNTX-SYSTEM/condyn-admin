# Recursive Perturbation

---

## Architectural Position

Recursive perturbation defines:
how topology changes
across recursive time.

---

## Perturbation Sources

Perturbations may emerge from:

- user interaction
- routing shifts
- latency variation
- participation change
- drift accumulation

---

## Perturbation Constraints

Perturbation remains:

- bounded
- observable
- temporally indexed

Perturbation may NOT:
inject semantic ontology.

---

## Recursive Principle

Small perturbations may:
propagate recursively
through field topology.

---

## Stability Requirement

Recursive perturbation remains stable only if:

ρ(J) < 1

---

## Final Statement

Recursive perturbation preserves:
dynamic adaptability
inside bounded topology.
